How It Works

1

Call Us

Pick up the phone and give us a ring. Tell us what's going on with your computer. No technical jargon needed.

2

We Connect

If we need to see your screen, we'll walk you through installing a small, free program called RustDesk. It lets us see and control your computer temporarily so we can fix the problem together.

3

We Fix It

We'll work through the issue with you, explain what's happening, and get you back on track. Most problems can be solved in one call.

4

Pass It On

If we helped you and you're able, consider giving back. Even $5/month on Patreon goes a long way. And the recording of your call might help someone else with the same problem when it goes up on YouTube.

What Kind of Help Can I Get?

Computer Running Slow

We'll figure out what's bogging it down and help speed things up. No expensive software needed.

Virus or Malware Worries

Think something's wrong? We'll take a look and help you clean things up using free, trusted tools.

Software Setup

Need help installing or setting up a program? We'll walk you through it step by step.

Printer, Email, Wi-Fi

The everyday stuff that drives everyone crazy. We've seen it all and we're happy to help.

Moving to Linux

Curious about Linux? We can help you try it out or switch over. No pressure, just honest guidance.

Something Else?

If it involves a computer and you're stuck, give us a call. Worst case, we'll point you in the right direction.

Pay What You Can

Everyone deserves help when their computer breaks, not just people who can afford it. That's why this service is pay what you can. If that's zero, that's completely fine. You still get the same help, no guilt trip.

That's also why every call is recorded and published on YouTube. The recordings help other people with similar problems, and the ad revenue helps make up for the time spent doing community service. It's not just content for content's sake. It's how the service stays sustainable even when most people pay nothing.

If you appreciated the support and are able to give back, the best way is a small monthly contribution on Patreon (coming soon). A $5/month subscription keeps this running far better than a one-time tip. But one-time donations of any amount are welcome too (also coming soon).

No hidden fees. No upsells. No data harvesting.

This is a best-effort service. We'll do everything we can to help, but we can't guarantee we'll be able to fix every problem. We are not responsible for any data loss or system issues that may occur during a support session. While serious problems are extremely unlikely, there are no guarantees of system integrity.
