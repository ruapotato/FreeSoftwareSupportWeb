Where the Money Goes

In the interest of full transparency:

  • Server costs - the RustDesk relay server and this website
  • Phone line - a dedicated number for support calls
  • David's time - researching problems, taking calls, editing videos
  • Software and equipment - recording, editing, and publishing tools

That's it. No overhead, no office, no middle management. Every contribution goes directly to keeping this running.
