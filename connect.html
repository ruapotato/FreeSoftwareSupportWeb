Get Connected

Download RustDesk so we can see your screen and help you fix the problem.

What Is RustDesk?

RustDesk is a free, open-source program that lets us see your screen and control your mouse and keyboard, but only when you give us permission. Think of it like having a tech-savvy friend look over your shoulder, except they can actually click things for you.

It's safe, it's free, and when you close it, we lose access immediately. No spying, no staying connected in the background. You're always in control.

Step 1: Download RustDesk

Click the button below to go to the RustDesk download page. Choose the version that matches your computer (Windows, Mac, or Linux).

Download RustDesk

RustDesk is free and open-source software. It does not cost anything to download or use.

Step 2: Install and Open It

Once the download finishes:

1

Find the downloaded file. It's usually in your Downloads folder.

2

Double-click it to install. If your computer asks "are you sure?" just click Yes. It's safe.

3

Open RustDesk. You'll see a window with a big ID number and a password.

Step 3: Connect to Our Server

Click the button below to automatically configure RustDesk to connect to our support server. This only needs to be done once.

Configure RustDesk
What does this do? It tells RustDesk to connect through our private server instead of the public one. This keeps your connection secure and direct. If the button doesn't work, don't worry. We can walk you through the setup over the phone.

Step 4: Read Us the ID and Password

Once RustDesk is open and configured, you'll see two things on screen:

  • Your ID - a string of numbers at the top
  • Your Password - below the ID (click the eye icon to show it if hidden)

Just read both of those to us over the phone. That's all we need to connect.

● Reminder: Everything we do on your screen during the support session may be recorded and published on YouTube. We'll always tell you before we start recording.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is RustDesk safe?

Yes. RustDesk is open-source, meaning anyone can inspect the code. It doesn't install anything sneaky, and it only connects when you give someone your ID and password. When you close it, the connection is gone.

Can you access my computer after we hang up?

No. Once you close RustDesk or end the session, we have no access whatsoever. The password changes each time, so even if you leave RustDesk open, we can't reconnect without the new password.

Do I need to keep RustDesk installed?

Only if you think you might call again. You can uninstall it any time. There's nothing it leaves behind.

What if the configure button doesn't work?

No problem. Just call us and we'll walk you through entering the server information manually. It takes about 30 seconds.
